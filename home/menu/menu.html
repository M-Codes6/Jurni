About Jurni

Jurni is a modern library app designed to help users manage and explore their personal reading journeys. From tracking favorite books to discovering new recommendations, Jurni empowers readers to stay organized and inspired. Currently, the app is in its basic version and will become more feature-rich in the future. Developed by Muzamil (M_Codes6)
